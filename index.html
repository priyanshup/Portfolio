Scheduler-Framework: Key to Automated Success

At UHG, we faced a common challenge: how to streamline script execution without sacrificing time or security. That's when Anup Mittal [My then manager] and I stepped in, bringing to life the Scheduler-Framework – our in-house innovation designed to revolutionize how we work.

Together, we crafted a solution that not only meets our needs but exceeds them. The Scheduler-Framework allows us to schedule scripts effortlessly, ensuring precise timing and seamless execution chaining. No more late-night alarms or manual interventions – now, scripts run like clockwork, leaving us free to focus on what truly matters.

This scheduler empowered us to:
  1. Effortlessly Schedule Scripts: Define script names, execution times, and frequencies using familiar CRON syntax.
  2. Ensure Precise Timing: The framework monitors the clock, executing scripts precisely when needed, without manual intervention.
  3. Seamless Execution Chaining: Chain multiple scripts effortlessly, ensuring flawless execution one step at a time.
  4. Enhanced Data Security: Developed internally, the Scheduler-Framework safeguards sensitive information, eliminating risks associated with third-party vendors.
  5. Cost Savings: By developing the solution in-house, we've saved on third-party subscriptions, maximizing cost-efficiency.

The impact? Astounding. By automating script execution, we've reclaimed valuable time and resources, empowering our team to tackle bigger challenges with confidence.

Member Mockup Tool: Streamlined QA

In the fast-paced world of Quality Assurance at UHG, time is of the essence. But when faced with the challenge of mocking member information for testing purposes, our QA team encountered significant delays. The need to safeguard Personal Health Information (PHI) meant that traditional methods were time-consuming and impractical.

As part of my personal project and commitment to the team, I developed the Member Mockup Tool using Excel. This innovative tool revolutionized our testing process by automating the mockup of member data, saving valuable time and resources.

With the Member Mockup Tool, our QA team can:
  1. Efficiently Generate Mockup Data: Enter the desired number of members to be mocked, and the tool automatically selects random values for around 80 mandatory mockup fields from a predefined set.
  2. Automatically Generate Insert Scripts: Seamlessly generate insert scripts for the mocked-up data, ensuring easy integration into our testing environment.
  3. Prevent Data Duplication: The tool includes data cleanup scripts to prevent duplication of member data in the database, ensuring more accurate and reliable testing.

By introducing this, we reduced the turnaround time of testing by 45%. The Member Mockup Tool transformed the QA process, and enabled us to deliver high-quality results more efficiently than ever before.
