About Me

I’m Priyanshu Pushpam, a Product Owner with 9 years of experience building and scaling products across B2B, B2C, SaaS, and E-commerce. I started out as a developer, and that technical foundation still shapes how I approach product today — connecting business goals with execution so ideas don’t just stay in slides, but actually ship and make an impact.

Over the years, I’ve:

  • Led cross-functional teams and driven Agile adoption
  • Delivered solutions that improved efficiency, reduced costs, and scaled to 90,000+ SKUs
  • Worked across industries like Healthcare, Sportsbook, and E-commerce — learning how to adapt quickly and thrive in fast-changing environments

What drives me is curiosity. I love experimenting with AI, digging into data to uncover insights, and seeing how small product decisions can create outsized impact. For me, product management isn’t just about delivery — it’s about owning vision, driving outcomes, and building teams and products that last.

Looking ahead, I’m focused on growing into leadership roles where I can scale this impact further — building products that matter, and teams that thrive.

Scheduler-Framework: Automating Success at UHG

At UHG, manual script execution was eating up time and creating risks. Together with my then manager, Anup Mittal, we built the Scheduler-Framework – an in-house solution that transformed how we automate workflows.

The framework schedules scripts effortlessly, ensures precise timing, and allows seamless execution chaining. No more late-night interventions – scripts now run like clockwork, freeing the team to focus on high-impact work.

Key Benefits:
  1. Effortless Scheduling: Define script names, execution times, and frequencies with familiar CRON syntax.
  2. Precise Execution: Scripts run exactly when needed, eliminating manual monitoring.
  3. Seamless Chaining: Link multiple scripts for flawless step-by-step execution.
  4. Enhanced Security: Built in-house, the framework protects sensitive data without relying on third-party tools.
  5. Cost Efficiency: Saving on third-party subscriptions while gaining full control over automation.

The outcome? Significant time savings, higher reliability, and a team empowered to tackle more complex challenges with confidence.

Member Mockup Tool: QA Made Faster

In UHG's fast-paced QA environment, creating mock member data while protecting PHI was slow and error-prone. Traditional methods caused significant delays in testing workflows.

I developed the Member Mockup Tool in Excel to automate the creation of mock member data. This tool revolutionized our QA process, saving time and ensuring reliable, secure test data.

Key Benefits:
  1. Efficient Data Generation: Automatically create any number of mock members with ~80 mandatory fields populated from predefined sets.
  2. Seamless Script Generation: Generate insert scripts directly for integration into testing environments.
  3. Duplicate-Free Data: Built-in cleanup scripts prevent duplication, ensuring accuracy and reliability.

With this tool, our QA team reduced testing turnaround time by 45%, delivering high-quality results faster and more efficiently than ever before.
